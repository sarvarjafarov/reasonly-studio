Frequently asked questions

Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.
What happens when I run out of AI prompts?
Your dashboards will continue to work, but AI-generated insights will be paused until the next billing cycle. You can upgrade your plan for more prompts anytime.
Do you offer a free trial?
The Free plan is available forever with no credit card required. Pro plan comes with a 14-day free trial so you can test all features before committing.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and offer invoicing for Enterprise customers.
Can I add more ad accounts to my plan?
Yes! If you need more ad accounts than your current plan allows, you can either upgrade to a higher tier or contact us for custom pricing.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, secure OAuth connections, and never store your ad platform credentials. Your data is backed up daily and stored securely.
