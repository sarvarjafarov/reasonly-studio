What is Google Search Console?

Google Search Console is a free tool from Google that helps you monitor, maintain, and troubleshoot your website's presence in Google Search results. It provides crucial insights into how Google sees your site and how users find you through organic search.

Key Metrics We Track

  • Search Performance: Total clicks, impressions, average CTR, average position
  • Query Analysis: Which search queries bring users to your site
  • Page Performance: Top landing pages from organic search
  • Geographic Data: Which countries drive the most organic traffic
  • Device Breakdown: Mobile, desktop, and tablet performance
  • Search Appearance: Rich results, AMP pages, and other SERP features

Why Combine Search Console with Ad Data?

Most businesses manage organic and paid search separately, missing critical insights:

  • Budget Optimization: Reduce paid spend on keywords you already rank well for organically
  • Keyword Strategy: Use organic data to find new paid keyword opportunities
  • Complete Picture: Understand total search visibility (organic + paid) for important queries
  • Content Planning: Identify high-value topics based on both organic and paid performance
  • Competitive Analysis: See where you're winning organically vs. where you need paid support

Insights You'll Get

  • Pages with high organic impressions but low clicks (need optimization or paid boost)
  • Keywords where you're paying for clicks despite ranking organically
  • Seasonal trends in organic search that should inform paid campaigns
  • Content gaps where competitors rank but you don't
  • Mobile vs. desktop performance differences across organic and paid
