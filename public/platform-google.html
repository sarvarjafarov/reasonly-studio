What is Google Ads?

Google Ads is the world's largest online advertising platform, reaching billions of people across Google Search, YouTube, Gmail, and millions of partner websites. It's essential for businesses looking to capture high-intent customers actively searching for their products or services.

Key Metrics We Track

  • Search Performance: Clicks, impressions, CTR, average position
  • Conversion Tracking: Conversion rate, cost per conversion, conversion value
  • Cost Analysis: CPC, CPM, total spend, budget utilization
  • Quality Metrics: Quality Score, expected CTR, ad relevance
  • Shopping Campaigns: Product performance, ROAS, cart abandonment
  • Video Campaigns: View rate, watch time, earned actions

Campaign Types Supported

  • Search Campaigns: Text ads on Google search results
  • Display Network: Banner and native ads across partner sites
  • Shopping Campaigns: Product listings for e-commerce
  • Video Campaigns: YouTube ads (skippable, non-skippable, bumper)
  • Performance Max: Google's AI-powered campaign type
  • App Campaigns: Mobile app install and engagement ads

Easy Setup

Connect your Google Ads account in minutes:

  • Go to Integrations in your Reasonly dashboard
  • Select Google Ads and sign in with your Google account
  • Choose which ad accounts to connect
  • Data syncs automatically every hour

Security & Privacy

We use Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure, read-only access. Reasonly can view your campaign data but cannot create, edit, or delete campaigns. We never see your Google password.
