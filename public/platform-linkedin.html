What is LinkedIn Ads?

LinkedIn Ads is the premier B2B advertising platform, connecting you with over 900 million professionals worldwide. It's the most effective platform for reaching decision-makers, generating quality leads, and building brand authority in the professional space.

Key Metrics We Track

  • Lead Generation: Lead form submissions, cost per lead, lead quality scores
  • Engagement: Clicks, CTR, social actions, followers gained
  • Conversions: Website visits, content downloads, demo requests
  • Cost Metrics: CPC, CPM, cost per send (Sponsored InMail)
  • Audience Insights: Job function, seniority, company size, industry
  • Content Performance: Which ad formats and messages resonate with professionals

Ad Formats Supported

  • Sponsored Content: Native ads in the LinkedIn feed (single image, video, carousel)
  • Message Ads: Direct messages to prospects' LinkedIn inbox
  • Text Ads: Simple PPC ads on LinkedIn sidebar
  • Dynamic Ads: Personalized ads using LinkedIn profile data
  • Lead Gen Forms: Pre-filled forms for easy lead capture

Why LinkedIn Ads Matter for B2B

LinkedIn is fundamentally different from consumer-focused platforms:

  • Target by job title, function, seniority, and company - impossible on other platforms
  • Reach decision-makers when they're in a professional mindset
  • Higher CPCs but significantly better lead quality for B2B
  • Native Lead Gen Forms with auto-populated professional information
  • Trust and credibility from LinkedIn's professional environment

Easy Setup

Connect your LinkedIn Campaign Manager:

  • Go to Integrations in your Reasonly account
  • Select LinkedIn Ads and log in with your LinkedIn credentials
  • Grant read-only access to your ad accounts
  • Data syncs automatically - no CSV exports needed

Security & Privacy

We use LinkedIn's OAuth protocol for secure authentication. Reasonly receives read-only access to your campaign metrics - we cannot create, modify, or delete campaigns. Your LinkedIn password remains private.
