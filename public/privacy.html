Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

At AdsData, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for AdsData, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Company information (for business accounts)
  • Username and password
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

Advertising Platform Data

When you connect your advertising accounts, we access:

  • Campaign performance metrics and statistics
  • Ad spend and budget information
  • Audience and targeting data
  • Ad creative performance (metadata only, not actual creative files)

Usage Information

We collect information about how you use AdsData:

  • Dashboard views and interactions
  • Features you use most often
  • Time spent on the platform
  • Device and browser information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our services: Display your advertising data in unified dashboards
  • Generate insights: Use AI to analyze your data and provide recommendations
  • Improve our platform: Understand how users interact with AdsData to make improvements
  • Send updates: Notify you about important changes, new features, or security issues
  • Process payments: Handle billing and subscription management
  • Provide support: Respond to your questions and troubleshoot issues

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • OAuth authentication: We never store your advertising platform passwords
  • Access controls: Strict internal access policies and role-based permissions
  • Regular backups: Your data is backed up daily to prevent loss
  • Security monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting (Heroku), analytics, and payment processors who help us operate
  • Advertising platforms: We access your data through official APIs with your explicit permission
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect: Revoke access to your advertising platforms at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • Personal information is deleted within 30 days
  • Advertising data is deleted immediately
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years for legal compliance

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymous)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

AdsData is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or through the platform.

Contact Us
