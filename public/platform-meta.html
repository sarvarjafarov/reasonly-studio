What is Meta Ads?

Meta Ads (formerly Facebook Ads) is the advertising platform for Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and the Meta Audience Network. With over 3 billion monthly active users across Meta's family of apps, it's one of the most powerful advertising platforms for reaching your target audience.

What AdsData Does for Your Meta Ads

Performance Metrics

Key Metrics We Track

  • Reach & Impressions: How many people see your ads
  • Engagement: Likes, comments, shares, and click-through rates
  • Conversions: Purchases, sign-ups, and custom conversion events
  • Cost Metrics: CPC, CPM, CPA, and total ad spend
  • ROAS: Return on ad spend to measure campaign profitability
  • Audience Insights: Demographics, locations, and device breakdowns

Easy Setup

Connecting your Meta Ads account takes less than 2 minutes:

  • Click "Connect Platform" in your AdsData dashboard
  • Select Meta Ads and log in with your Facebook account
  • Grant AdsData read-only access to your ad accounts
  • Your data syncs automatically - no manual exports needed

Security & Privacy

We use OAuth authentication, which means we never see or store your Facebook password. AdsData only requests read-only access to your advertising data - we can't create, modify, or delete your campaigns.
