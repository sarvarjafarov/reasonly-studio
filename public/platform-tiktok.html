What is TikTok Ads?

TikTok Ads is the advertising platform for TikTok, one of the fastest-growing social media apps with over 1 billion active users. It's the go-to platform for brands targeting younger audiences with short-form video content that drives engagement and conversions.

Key Metrics We Track

  • Video Metrics: Views, watch time, completion rate, average watch percentage
  • Engagement: Likes, comments, shares, profile visits, follows
  • Conversions: App installs, purchases, sign-ups, and custom events
  • Cost Performance: CPM, CPC, CPV, CPA, and total ad spend
  • Audience Data: Age, gender, location, device, and interest breakdowns
  • Creative Performance: Which video formats and hooks perform best

Ad Formats Supported

  • In-Feed Ads: Native video ads in users' For You feed
  • TopView: Premium placement when users open TikTok
  • Spark Ads: Boost organic posts for authentic engagement
  • Collection Ads: Showcase multiple products in a single ad
  • Branded Hashtag Challenges: User-generated content campaigns

Why Track TikTok Ads?

TikTok's algorithm and audience behavior are unique. What works on Facebook or Instagram won't necessarily work here. Reasonly helps you:

  • Identify which video hooks and formats drive the most engagement
  • Understand your cost per result compared to other platforms
  • Track trending content styles that resonate with your audience
  • Optimize for TikTok's unique metrics like video completion rate

Easy Setup

Connect your TikTok Ads Manager account:

  • Navigate to Integrations in your Reasonly dashboard
  • Select TikTok Ads and log in with your TikTok account
  • Authorize read-only access to your ad accounts
  • Your campaigns sync automatically

Security & Privacy

We use TikTok's official OAuth authentication for secure access. Reasonly only requests read permissions - we cannot create or modify your campaigns. Your TikTok password is never shared with us.
